Your Local Spot for fresh water tropical fish!

What began as a quarantine hobby in the basement of my home in Parker, Colorado turned quickly into a flourishing business that allows me to share my love of fish. Vibrant Tropical is a locally-owned small business that delivers healthy and beautiful cichlids to your doorstep with just the click of a button.

How It Works

Our process is simple. In just a few short steps, we'll have your new fish out the door and delivered safely right to your doorstep.

Step 1

Choose your new fish friends

Step 2

Purchase on our site

Step 3

A driver from www.roadie.com picks up your fish

Step 4

Your fish are delivered to your home by end of day!
